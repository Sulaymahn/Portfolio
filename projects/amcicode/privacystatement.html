Privacy Policy for Amci Code

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines how AMCI Code ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Information We Collect We collect only one type of information:

Matriculation Number: We automatically collect and store the matriculation number you input into the App. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

How We Use Your Information We use the matriculation number you provide solely for the purpose of retrieving AMCI (and related) details, making the process more convenient for you. We do not access, share, or use this information for any other purpose.

Data Storage and Security Your matriculation number is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to or store this information on our servers. We take reasonable measures to secure your data within the App to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

Data Deletion If you choose to clear data or uninstall the App, all stored matriculation number data will be permanently deleted from your device, and we will have no access to it.

Children's Privacy The AMCI Code App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us, and we will promptly delete such information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes to this Policy by posting a notice within the App. Your continued use of the App after the posting of any changes to this Policy signifies your acceptance of those changes.
